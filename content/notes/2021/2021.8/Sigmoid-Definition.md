# Sigmoid

<div align="right"> 2021-08-19</div>

Tags: #Sigmoid

Sigmoid means resembling the lower-case Greek letter sigma (uppercase Σ, lowercase σ, lowercase in word-final position ς) or the Latin letter S[^1]
