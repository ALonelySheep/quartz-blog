# Difference between Purple and Violet

<div align="right"> 2022-04-23</div>

Tags: #Purple #Violet #English #Color

- Purple是复色光(Blue and Red 1:1)激发的颜色, 而Violet是单色光激发的颜色 
- 因而看起来Purple要Red一点, 饱和度要高一点.
